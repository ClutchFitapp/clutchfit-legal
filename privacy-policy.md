ClutchFit Privacy Policy
Effective Date: June 18, 2026
This Privacy Policy applies to the ClutchFit mobile application (the "Application"), operated by Edward Johnson ("Service Provider," "we," "us," or "our"). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use ClutchFit.
Information We Collect
When you use ClutchFit, we may collect the following personally identifiable information: Email address, Username, User ID, Age/Date of Birth, Biological Sex/Gender, Height, Weight, Body Fat Percentage, Workout History, Exercise Logs, Sets/Reps/Weight Lifted, Personal Records, RPE Ratings, Readiness Scores, Nutrition Logs, Water Intake, Weekly Body Weight, Device Identifiers, App Usage Patterns, Notification Preferences, Subscription Status, Friends List, Challenge Participation, Activity Feed Posts, Encouragements Sent and Received, Subscription Tier, and Profile Photo.
We may also automatically collect your device's IP address, mobile operating system, the pages of the Application you visit, and the time spent using the Application.
Camera and AI Food Scanner: If you use the AI food scanner feature, photos you take are transmitted to our AI processing partner (OpenAI) to identify food items and estimate nutritional content. These photos are processed for that purpose and are not permanently stored by ClutchFit after analysis.
Health and Fitness Data: Data such as your weight, body fat percentage, workout history, and nutrition logs is used solely to power app features — including AI program generation, nutrition goal calculations, and progress tracking. This data is never sold to third parties.
Third-Party Services
ClutchFit relies on the following third-party services to operate. Each has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data:

Supabase — database, authentication, and storage
OpenAI — AI food scanning and program generation
RevenueCat — subscription and payment processing
Resend — transactional email delivery
Spoonacular — food and recipe database
Expo — application platform
Google Sign-In / Google Play Services — authentication and platform services

Only the minimum data necessary is shared with each service to provide its function. We do not sell your personal information.
Cookies and Tracking
The Application or its third-party SDKs may use cookies, SDKs, or similar technologies to support functionality and service delivery. Where required by law, we will obtain consent before using non-essential tracking technologies.
Your Rights
You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at clutchfitapp@gmail.com.
California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)
California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete it, the right to opt out of its sale or sharing, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. Contact us at clutchfitapp@gmail.com to exercise these rights.
Artificial Intelligence
ClutchFit uses AI to power the food scanner and AI workout program generation. AI processing is performed in accordance with this policy and applicable law.
International Data Transfers
Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. Where required, we apply appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or other legally recognized transfer mechanisms.
Data Retention

User Provided Data: Retained for the duration of your account plus 12 months after deletion, unless longer retention is legally required.
Automatically Collected Data: Retained for up to 24 months.
Aggregated/Anonymized Data: Retained indefinitely as it no longer identifies you.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting clutchfitapp@gmail.com, subject to data required for legal compliance.
Children's Privacy
ClutchFit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us information should contact clutchfitapp@gmail.com.
Security
We use physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
Data Breach Notification
If a breach affecting your personal data occurs, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.
Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be posted with a new effective date, and where required by law, we will seek your consent before they take effect.
Contact Us
Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to clutchfitapp@gmail.com.
